Services

Our Features

Electrum Zcash is more than just a wallet. We provide cutting-edge solutions to streamline cryptocurrency management, from fast transactions to comprehensive security measures.

All services

Multi-Signature Security

Strengthen your protection with multi-signature capabilities, which require several private keys for transaction authorization. This option is perfect for shared accounts or additional security layers.

Real-Time Transaction Monitoring

Monitor your transactions instantly, with notifications for successful sends, receives, and confirmations. Stay informed about your wallet activity without the hassle of constant checking.

Encrypted Backup Solutions

Manage your wallet data to keep your assets safe and recoverable in case of device loss or failure. Protect your cryptocurrency with encrypted backups you control, ensuring you don’t have to worry about losing your funds.

Cross-Device Syncing

Access your wallet across multiple devices, keeping your funds within reach and transactions synchronized. Easily manage your Zcash on any device, whether at home or at work.
