ABOUT ELECTRUM ZEC

Electrum ZEC is an innovative wallet designed to empower users in managing their Zcash securely and efficiently. Built on the foundation of the original Electrum wallet, which was created by Thomas Voegtlin in November 2011, Electrum ZEC carries forward a legacy of excellence and community collaboration.

Commitment to Security

At Electrum ZEC, we prioritize the security of our users and the integrity of our software. Developers do not have direct, independent write access to the website. Instead, all changes must be signed by both Thomas Voegtlin and SomberNight, ensuring a double layer of verification.

Before any changes become publicly visible, they are automatically checked by a dedicated script, safeguarding against unauthorized modifications.
